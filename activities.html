A Foundation for Lifelong Learning

At Pratham Pathshala, we offer a well-rounded and engaging curriculum designed to foster the holistic development of every child. Our curriculum combines creative activities, hands-on learning, and foundational skills to prepare children for future academic success.

Key highlights of our curriculum include:

Foundational Learning: Introduction to letters, numbers, shapes, and colors through fun and interactive activities.

Language Development: Encouraging the use of simple English sentences to build communication skills and self-expression.

Environmental Awareness: Learning about nature, animals, and surroundings to spark curiosity and understanding of the world.

Social and Moral Values: Instilling respect, etiquette, and a sense of responsibility through daily interactions and guided lessons.

Parent-Teacher Partnership: Regular meetings to ensure personalized attention and consistent communication about a child’s progress and areas for improvement.

Weekly updates are provided to keep parents informed about their child’s learning journey and classroom activities. At Pratham Pathshala, we strive to create a nurturing environment where young minds thrive!

Our Activities

Educational Field Trips

Educational field trips offer hands-on experiences that enhance classroom learning, fostering curiosity and observation skills. By connecting concepts to real life, these trips encourage critical thinking, social interaction, and personal growth.

Fairy Tale Day

Fairy Tale Day transports children into a magical world of stories and imagination, fostering creativity and confidence. Engaging activities and costumes make this enchanting day joyful and unforgettable.

Parent Orientation Activity

Parent Orientation Activity fosters collaboration by introducing parents to the school’s philosophy and their child’s learning journey. It builds trust, aligns expectations, and supports holistic growth.

Pool Day

Pool Day is a refreshing and fun-filled activity where children enjoy splashing around in a safe, supervised environment. It promotes relaxation, boosts physical development, and enhances coordination, making it a delightful way for kids to bond and have fun!

Health Checkups

Health Checkups are an essential part of ensuring every child’s well-being. Regular health camps conducted by specialists help monitor growth, identify potential issues early, and promote healthy habits. These checkups ensure that children stay happy, active, and ready to learn!

Children's Day Fete

A vibrant celebration filled with joy, laughter, and exciting activities, the Children's Day Fete honors the spirit of childhood. With games, performances, and special treats, it’s a day where children create cherished memories and embrace the joy of being young!

Plantation Drive

A meaningful initiative where children actively participate in planting trees, learning the value of nature and sustainability. This hands-on activity nurtures eco-awareness, responsibility, and a sense of contribution to a greener future!

Certificate Distribution

A special occasion recognizing and celebrating children's achievements, inspiring confidence and encouraging them to reach new milestones. Each certificate marks a step forward in their journey of growth and learning!

Creative Arts and Crafts

Creative arts and crafts encourage children to explore their creativity through painting, drawing, and DIY activities. These sessions enhance fine motor skills and foster innovative thinking.

Pratham Pathshala

Pratham Pathshala is committed to nurturing young minds in a safe, joyful, and stimulating environment. We provide a strong foundation for children aged 2–6 years, fostering creativity, confidence, and lifelong learning in every little learner!
